Reach

2015

Reach is an analytics suite that allows exploration of trends and interactions between users on Twitter. Reach provides the three main services:
1) User Analytics: High­ level summaries of any user's interactions.
2.) Trend Geography: Visualizations of how a trend propagates geographically as a function of time.
3) Trend Influencers: Identification of users who are influential about a specific topic.

Genome Simulator

2014

Worked in conjunction with Professor Itsik Pe'er and his lab in building a Genome Simulator--a software which provides user interactive 3D visual representations of simulations depicting genetic descent of populations across clearly defined generations. It takes in a genomic simulation represented by a self-developed language, parses it, visualizes and allows users to build upon the simulation.
