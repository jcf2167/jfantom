reach

Reach is a comprehensive analytics suite that allows you to explore surfacing trends and interactions between users on Twitter. Reach provdes the three main services:

1) User Analytics: Reach gives high­ level summaries of any Twitter user's interactions on the platform, providing more transparency to social media managers and publicists. User analytics can also be used by recruiters, political analysts, or anyone else who wishes to run a background check.

2.) Trend Geography: Reach visualizes how a trend on Twitter propagates geographically as a function of time.

3) Trend Influencers: Reach identifies Twitter users who are influential about a specific topic.

the buff plan

The Buff Plan provides customized exercise, nutrition and supplement plans for users based on the individual’s goals: weight loss, weight gain, weight maintenance and bodybuilding. The app takes into consideration: age, body weight, gender, height, activity level, exercise availability, favorite ingredients, dietary constraints and injuries. It also serves up recipes for foods based on a user's available ingredients/food along with their plan and restrictions.

Genome Simulator

A Genome Simulator tests hypotheses about sequence variation caused by demography patterns with real genetic data influenced by historical and evolutionary causes. The simulator helps in population genetics research by replicating events like the convergence or divergence of populations and migrations across generations. The simulator takes in a genomic simulation represented by a self-developed language and visualizes the simulation and provides user interactivity.
